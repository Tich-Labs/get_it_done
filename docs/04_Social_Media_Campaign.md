## Brand Voice Guidelines

✓ **DO:**
- Be authentic and personal ("We built this because...")
- Use numbers and real wins ("4 bookings in week 1")
- Be humble ("We're learning too")
- Ask questions (show genuine curiosity)
- Celebrate consultants (they're the heroes)

✗ **DON'T:**
- Use corporate jargon ("leverage," "synergy," "disrupt")
- Make claims without proof ("Change your life!")
- Ignore comments or feedback
- Over-promote (build community first)
- Forget local context (Kenya-first, always)
